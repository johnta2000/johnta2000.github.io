Notes

Chapters 1-2 are pretty much review, but make sure you understand the ideas behind molecular orbital theory extensively (including drawing MO diagrams, drawing single, double, and triple bond structures...) Chapter 3 is new material, but should be relatively easy to go through. Chapter 4 will be a mix of new and old material emphasizing bond energy and other related thermodynamics things. Chapter 5 is likely completely new, and is your first interesting glance into an innate part of chemistry as a whole. Chapter 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, and 11 are probably the most difficult part of this course, with the overall discussion tailored around reactions of molecules and their mechanisms. Chapter 12 and 13 are much different from the previous chapters and circle around spectroscopy.
