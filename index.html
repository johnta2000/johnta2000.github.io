About Me

I am an undergraduate pre-med student at the University of Pennsylvania in the Roy and Diana Vagelos Scholars Program in the Molecular Life Sciences studying Chemistry and Biochemistry at the College of Arts and Science. I come from Massachusetts, attending the Massachusetts Academy of Math and Science, and Saint John's for high school. My extracurricular interests include powerlifting, soccer, and web development.

Powerlifting

I have always hit the gym periodically throughout my life, but in January 2018, I decided that I would step up my game and began going to lift five, six, and seven days a week. I started off my journey with mediocre lifting numbers--bench, 115 lbs; deadlift, 215 lbs; squat, 205 lbs. Almost a year later, my numbers have skyrocketed, and I began to garner an interest in powerlifting. I am hoping to use this page to visually track my progression and to inspire others to join me.
